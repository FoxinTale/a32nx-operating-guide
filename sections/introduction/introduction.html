The A320 family consits of the Airbus A318, A319, A320, and the A321. Neo variants of the 320 and 321 also exist. Airbus aircraft operate on a significantly different principle that most flight simmers may be used to. Where as others put the control in the hands of the pilot, Airbus aircraft rely more on automation to reduce the chance of pilot error.

There are four golden principles any would be simmer flying an Airbus should know:

  1. Fly / Aviate, Navigate, Communicate. In this order. Always fly the plane first.
  2. Know the appropriate level of automation. Don't constantly rely on it. It can lie to you.
  3. Know your Flight Mode Annunciation at all times.
  4. If the automation isn't doing what you want, shut it off and take over.
